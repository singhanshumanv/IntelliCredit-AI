<h2>рџ“Њ Project Overview</h2>

<p>
<strong>IntelliCredit AI</strong> is an intelligent credit evaluation system that automates the process of analyzing financial documents and generating credit decisions using Artificial Intelligence.
</p>

<p>
Traditional credit underwriting requires analysts to manually review financial reports, assess risk indicators, and prepare credit appraisal reports before loan approval. This process is slow, resource-intensive, and prone to inconsistencies.
</p>

<p>
IntelliCredit AI leverages <strong>Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)</strong>, financial analytics, and explainable AI to automate the credit assessment pipeline and provide fast, data-driven lending insights.
</p>
